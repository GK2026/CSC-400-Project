Calculate the Correlation

Use Excel or Google Sheets to calculate the correlation between your two indicators.

How to calculate in Excel or Google Sheets:

1. Open your downloaded dataset
2. Put all X values in one column and all Y values in another
3. In an empty cell, type: =CORREL(A2:A20, B2:B20) Adjust the range to match your data
4. The result is your correlation value. It will be between −1 and 1

Tip: A value close to 1 means strong positive correlation, close to −1 means strong negative, close to 0 means little to no correlation.
